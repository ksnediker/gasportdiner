The Gasport Diner

The Gasport Diner opened in June 2016 and serves classic American food in a comfortable diner setting. The restaurant is located in the heart of Gasport, NY just steps from the Erie Canal. Breakfast and lunch are served daily and dinner is available on Friday evenings.
